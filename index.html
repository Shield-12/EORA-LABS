About Eora Labs

How I Approach Technology

Technology exists at the intersection of people, processes, infrastructure, security, and business operations. A password reset is also an identity-management event. A printer outage can become a workflow interruption. A domain join introduces trust, policy, naming, permissions, and administrative control.

Eora Labs documents that perspective. Its purpose is to show not only which tools I have used, but how I analyze systems, communicate technical risk, document operations, and design toward reliability.
