About

How I Think About Technology

Technology interests me because it sits at the intersection of people, processes, infrastructure, and security. A password reset is not just a ticket. It is identity lifecycle management. A printer outage is not just a device failure. It is workflow interruption. A domain join is not just a button click. It is trust, policy, naming, permissions, and operational readiness.

Signal & Systems exists to document that mindset. I want hiring managers to see not only what tools I have touched, but how I reason through systems, communicate risk, and build toward reliability.
